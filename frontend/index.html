About
The Story Behind
PropCare AI

PropCare AI was born from something I kept hearing over and over — property owners in Puerto Rico saying the same thing: "It's too much to keep up with." Whether it was finding a reliable cleaner between guests, tracking down a plumber on short notice, or just remembering when the last electrical inspection was done — managing a property here isn't simple. And for Airbnb hosts dealing with constant guest turnover, it can feel like a full-time job.

But what really made it click for me was personal. When my girlfriend and I moved into our current apartment, we learned it had originally been renovated as an Airbnb. The owner had put real work into it — but after a short time, the constant maintenance, the scheduling, the back-and-forth with service providers every single week became too much. She made the decision to switch to long-term rentals instead, simply because it was easier to manage.

That moment stuck with me. A well-maintained property, a willing owner — but no good tool to make the management side bearable. I thought: what if there was a platform that handled all of that? One place where you add your properties, describe what's wrong in plain words, and get real local help instantly? That's exactly what PropCare AI is.

Development began in early 2026 and is ongoing. This project was built as a Portfolio Project for 🎓 Holberton School — a project-based software engineering program, and represents everything I've learned about full-stack development, AI integration, and building tools that solve real problems for real people.
